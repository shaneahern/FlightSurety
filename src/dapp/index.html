To purchase insurance:
1. Click "Load Flights"
2. Select a flight
3. Select a passenger
4. Enter desired insurance purchase amount (up to 1 ETH)
5. Click "Buy Insurance"
6. Click "Load Passenger Balance" to see insurance balance and payout credit (prints to console)

To check flight status with Oracles:
1. Click "Submit to Oracles" button
2. Click "Check Oracles Response" button
3. Look at section below under "Oracle Response". If status is STATUS_CODE_LATE_AIRLINE, passengers on that flight will be credited the value of 1.5 times the amount of insurnce they purchased.

To transfer insurance payout balance to passengers:
1. Select the flight and passenger.
2. Click "Load Passenger Balance" to verify that they have a balance in "payoutCredit" (prints to console)
3. Click "Withdraw Insurance Payout" and balance of payoutCredit will be transfered to the passenger.
4. Click "Load Passenger Balance" to verify that the payoutCredit balance is now 0
5. Verify that the payoutCredit amount has been transfered to the passengers ETH wallet.
